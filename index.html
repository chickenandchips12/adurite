Connect Roblox Account

Paste your .ROBLOSECURITY cookie to connect. Get it from your browser while logged into Roblox:

  1. Go to roblox.com and log in
  2. Open DevTools (F12) → Application → Cookies → https://www.roblox.com
  3. Find _ROBLOSECURITY (or .ROBLOSECURITY)
  4. Double-click the Value to select all, then Ctrl+C to copy
  5. Paste here immediately — cookies expire when you log out

⚠️ Never share your cookie with anyone. We use it only to verify your account and fetch your inventory. We never store it.

If you get "Roblox rejected" despite a valid cookie, Roblox may block cloud servers. Try running locally: npm start → http://localhost:3000
